COPYCAT

Privacy Policy

Effective July 23, 2026

COPYCAT is a multiplayer party game that runs inside ChatGPT. We designed it to work without a separate COPYCAT account, advertising, analytics, or an operator-side model API.

Data we process

  • Game profile: a nickname chosen by you, your selected language, and a random pseudonymous player identifier stored by the widget.
  • Game activity: room code, hints, votes, scores, connection state, and any per-player hint translations needed to run the current game.
  • Technical delivery data: network information such as an IP address may be processed transiently by our hosting provider, Cloudflare, to deliver HTTPS and WebSocket traffic and protect the service.

We do not request your ChatGPT password, OpenAI API key, precise location, contacts, payment information, or full chat history. ChatGPT sign-in and model processing are handled by OpenAI under its own terms and privacy policy; COPYCAT does not receive your ChatGPT credentials.

How we use data

We use the information above only to match players, maintain realtime rooms, show the game in each participant's language, calculate scores, reconnect an interrupted player, prevent malformed requests, and operate the service.

Sharing and service providers

Room participants see the nickname, public hint, vote status, score, and language-dependent translations needed for play. Secret roles and the secret word are disclosed only as required by the rules. Cloudflare hosts the Worker, static assets, Durable Objects, and WebSocket connections. OpenAI hosts ChatGPT and invokes the MCP tools you choose to use. We do not sell personal information or share it for advertising.

Retention

Matchmaking queue entries expire after two minutes and match-result tickets after five minutes. Room data is automatically deleted no later than 24 hours after the room's last activity. A random player identifier, nickname, and language preference may remain in the widget's local storage until you clear the app or browser site data.

Your choices

You may use a non-identifying nickname. Closing the widget ends its realtime connection. You can clear locally stored preferences through ChatGPT or browser site-data controls. For a privacy question or deletion request made before automatic expiry, contact support with the four-character room code; do not send passwords or other sensitive information.

Children

COPYCAT is intended for a general audience aged 13 and older and is not directed to children under 13.

Changes and contact

Material changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date. For privacy or support questions, open an issue through our public support page.
